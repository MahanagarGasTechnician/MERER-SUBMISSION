About Our Submission Process

Customers using gas must capture two meter readings (before and after a 30‑second wait) along with a gas flame photo to confirm usage. Those not using gas need to capture a meter photo and a photo of the gas pipe. If your meter is disconnected, please provide a brief description along with a photo of the meter location.

How to Submit Your Gas Status

1. Using Gas (Active Usage)

When using gas, you must capture three photos in this order:

  1. Step 1: Meter’s First Photo

    Position your camera to clearly capture your gas meter’s display.

  2. Step 2: Gas Flame Photo

    Capture a photo of the gas flame. Make sure the flame is clearly visible.

  3. Step 3: Timer & Meter’s Second Photo

    After capturing the gas flame photo, wait for a 30‑second timer to complete. Then capture a second photo of your gas meter’s display.

Once all three photos have been captured, click the Submit button.

2. Not Using Gas (Inactive Usage)

If you are not using gas, follow these steps:

  1. Step 1: Meter Photo

    Capture a photo of your gas meter’s display.

  2. Step 2: Pipe Photo (Disconnected/Capped Pipe)

    Capture a photo of the gas pipe that is disconnected or capped.

After capturing both photos, click the Submit button to complete the process.

3. Meter Disconnected

If your gas meter is disconnected, follow these steps:

  1. Step 1: Provide a Brief Description

    In the provided text box, describe the situation.
    Example: "Meter was temporarily disconnected due to maintenance work."

  2. Step 2: Capture a Photo of the Meter’s Location

    Use your camera to take a photo of where the meter is (or used to be) located.

Once you have written your description and captured the photo, click the Submit button to finish the process.

Instructions for Taking Clear Photos:

  • Hold your device steady.
  • Make sure there’s enough light.
  • Check that all details (like numbers on the meter) are clearly visible.
